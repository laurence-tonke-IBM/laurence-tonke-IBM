## Every misuse a technology allows *will* happen.

Ever heard of Murphy’s Law? "Anything that can go wrong *will* go wrong."

I think there is another law like this, especially when we are talking about new technologies: "Every misuse a technology allows will happen."
Whenever a new technology is invented, there are already people imagining what they can do with it. And not only the ggod things. In this area, the human brain is much faster than AI. And it's much more creative.

Scientists discovered nuclear fission, yet the atomic bomb was invented before the first nuclear power plants even came online.
Software developers created platforms for AI-powered video editing, and some complete idiots are using them to create deepfake videos.
"Every misuse a technology allows *will* happen."

But what are the consequences? Does that mean we should stop investing in new technologies?
No, it doesn't. That wouldn't achieve anything. Others would simply do it instead.

This means handling technology responsibly. Making technology secure. Leading by example. Taking responsibility.
Any misuse that a technology allows will inevitably occur.
You cannot prevent it, but you can lead by example.

Do the right thing.
